Patna Women’s College is a constituent college of Patna University. It was established in 1940 to impart quality education in the fields of Arts, Science, Commerce, Management and Computer Applications at UG and PG level. It was the first institution of higher learning opened to cater the educational needs of women in Bihar.At present, Patna Women’s College offers BA, B.Sc, B.Com, BBA. BCA, BMC, B.Ed, MA, MCA and PG Diploma courses in various disciplines.

  • Admission to all UG, PG and PG Diploma courses is based on the Patna University Common Entrance Test (PUCET) conducted by the college followed by an Interview and counselling.
  • Final admission confirmation is subject to course-wise merit list and availability of seats.
  • Final admission to B.Ed is subject to counselling conducted by Patna University.
  • There is no entrance exam for MA (Home Science) program. Candidates are selected on the basis of their marks in the last qualifying examination.
  • Admission form can be filled online followed by final submission at college. The application fee can be paid By Credit/ Debit Card or through Challan.

Patna Women’s College Application Process 2022

Patna Women’s College accepts applications from eligible candidates through online mode only. The application process is as follows:

  • 1. Visit the official website of Patna Women’s College.
  • 2. Click on ‘Admission Notice’ in the Notices section.
  • 3. On the next page, click on the ‘Application Form’ Link.
  • 4. Select your course level and Course for which you are seeking admission.
  • 5. Fill course and personal details and click on ‘Submit’.
  • 6. Pay application fee By Credit/ Debit Card or through Challan.
